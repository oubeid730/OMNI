Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13 August 2025

OmniBot respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data:

1. Data We Collect

  • User IDs and messages may be temporarily accessed for bot functionality (commands, logging, etc.)
  • Server information necessary for bot features may be stored
  • No sensitive personal data (like passwords) is collected
  • Command usage data may be collected for analytics and improvement purposes

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide and maintain bot features and respond to commands
  • To improve the bot's performance and fix errors
  • To analyze usage patterns for service improvement
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share user data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Data may be shared with service providers necessary for bot operation (e.g., hosting providers)
  • Data may be shared if required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Aggregate, non-personally identifiable data may be used for statistical analysis

4. Data Retention

  • Data is stored only as long as necessary for bot functionality
  • Most data is automatically deleted after 30 days unless required for ongoing operations
  • You may request data deletion by contacting us (see Section 6)

5. Changes to Policy

  • We may update this policy at any time
  • Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy
  • Significant changes will be announced through our official channels

6. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, join our support server.

By using OmniBot, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
